Simple Animations

  • You can describe an animation of the element by using animation tag and keyframe tags.
  • A style sheet of the element will be overwritten when animation.
<p style="width: 48px; height: 48px;

          background-color:#fdd" id="box1"></p>

<p style="width: 48px; height: 48px;

          background-color:#dfd" id="box2"></p>

<p style="width: 48px; height: 48px;

          background-color:#ddf" id="box3"></p>

<animation>

  <keyframe target="box1" css="width: 96px">

  <keyframe target="box2" css="height: 96px">

  <keyframe target="box3" css="width: 96px;  height: 96px">

</animation>

Advanced Animations

  • You can transition property if you with to animate the element smoothly.
  • Make your animation by combining style sheet :)
<p style="width: 48px;

          height: 48px;

          transition-duration: 1s;

          background-color:#dfd" id="box4"></p>

<p style="width: 48px;

          height: 48px;

          transition-duration: 1s;

          background-color:#ddf" id="box5"></p>

<animation>

  <keyframe target="box4" css="width: 96px;

                               background-color:red">

  <keyframe target="box5" css="-webkit-transform: rotate(90deg)">

</animation>
